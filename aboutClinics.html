Blossom Tennis

Our 10 & Under QuickStart Clinic is tailored to children ages 5 - 10. The primary focus of this program is to introduce the FUNdamentals of tennis along with athletic skills. Coaches will instruct basic stroke techniques while incorporating hand-eye coordination and footwork drills. The skills developed will prepare students to compete in junior tournaments and/or join one of our Junior Team Tennis teams to play during the spring and fall seasons.

The QuickStart program consists of eight classes offered twice per week, over four sessions throughout the 2022 Fall season. The cost is $125 per 4 weeks session. Price may vary due to holidays or scheduled tournaments. Mon/Wed Session 1 and 2 are pro-rated due to NEISD Holiday ( Sept. 5 and Oct. 10)

Choose between our Monday/Wednesday or Tuesday/Thursday options. Both are one hour in length and begin at 4:15 pm.

If the class of your choice is full, please join the wailtlist. We will do our best to accomodate you!

This Junior Tournament Clinic is geared toward junior players who are actively competing in USTA and UTRTournaments, or above. This class ensures a proper and technically sound foundation with special emphasis on strategy, tactics and patterns of play. Our coaches utilize innovative court drills, conditioning and supervised match play to develop player potential. We expect our junior players to commit to monthly Challenger or Champs tournaments in addition to local tournaments.
